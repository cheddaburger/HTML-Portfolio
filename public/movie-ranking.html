The Best Movies According to Stephen

My Top 3 Movies of all time.

From Up On Poppy Hill

The illustrations are beautiful and the story is incredibly touching. I love a good romatic movie.

The Other Guys

An amazing buddy cop movie that breaks down corruption in a simple way, while also being hilarious.

The Batman

An instant hit, something that I didnt know I was going love so much. It was one of the most thrilling movies I have ever seen.
